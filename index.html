EMP3

Android App

EMP3 stands for Emotion based Music Player for Android devices

It generates a playlist for users according to their mood, detected through facial expressions.

The app captures the user's image and detects face using Viola-Jones algorithm. It then uses the classifier developed using Fisherfaces algorithm to identify the user's emotion. It then creates a playlist based on the mood and manipulates the playlist in such a way that songs down the list will make the user feel more happier.

The app detects 3 emotions via happy, angry and sad.

Achieved 90% accuracy in human emotion detection.
